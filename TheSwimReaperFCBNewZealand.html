Patrolling Kiwi waterways since 2016.

Since 2016, the Swim Reaper has patrolled NZ’s swimming spots, ironically tempting young swimmers to be as dumb as possible.

Arguably NZ’s most successful social campaign ever, the Swim Reaper has amassed over 400,000 followers, made headlines worldwide and seen an 85% drop in drownings of young men.
