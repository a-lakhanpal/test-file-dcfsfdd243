What do you want to change?

Little changes add up to big shifts in attitudes and behaviour: changes that help grow business and profit - and even help change the shape of our society. But we don't make change for the sake of it. We're all about progressive change: making things better, from the smallest interaction, a bottom-line improvement - to helping our clients save lives.

We take our mahi seriously. Ourselves, not so much. Sure, awards are nice (and we have a more than a few in our trophy cabinet) but the real joy comes from pulling together as a team, working out a problem as an integrated group and knowing that every one of us will be heard. And we have fun while we do it. Otherwise, what’s the point, right?

FCB is an advocate for a diverse, equitable and inclusive culture for all members of our team. So if you’re looking for a place where you can show up to work every day as your truest and best self, freely expressing who you are, where you can create great work and also have a good time, we’re glad you stopped by.

Senior Account Manager : FCB Wellington Office
Opportunity

We have a rare and exciting opportunity here at FCB for the position of Senior Account Manager in our Account Management Team, Pōneke.
Located in our beautiful central Wellington office in Waring Taylor Street, this role will provide clear pathways  to continue to grow and develop your career in Account Management, while working alongside some of the best in the business, in arguably NZ’s finest fully integrated, behaviour change agency.  You’’ll be collaborating closely with our client partners, and other FCB’ers, producing effective, measurable, behaviour changing results. 

You

You are a slick account management professional – currently at SAM level or ready to step up into an SAM level position .  This role offers you the opportunity to flex your account management or project management skills in arguably NZ’s finest agency.  Utilising your demonstrated track record of superior and strong client relationship management at a senior level, the results will speak for themselves.
You have an innate understanding of the big picture, in addition to a keen eye for detail and organisation, and a passion for end to end project management.  At FCB, you’ll both love, and champion, great creative.  An eagerness to collaborate and contribute will be second nature.  Being both tenacious and resilient, you’ll be day-to-day key point of contact for your client, directing, guiding and advising on BAU.  Working in this tight-knit team, you’ll be willing to pitch in to get the mahi done, comfortable managing both up and down, using your initiative and naturally proactive approach.

About our FCB whānau

We’re famous for producing hard hitting, effective work and providing our high profile clients with innovative, award winning and effective campaigns. Within the industry, we’re well known for our culture, and we protect it like a precious taonga.
Between our offices in Wellington and Auckland, we are diverse, creative and proud of our people focussed culture & our supportive working environment. We live and breathe our company values which is evident in our approach to everything we do – Never finished, Be Generous and Brave Together.  The culture that we foster inspires passion and fierce loyalty.

You’ll need to be
  • Proactive, able to seek out opportunities drawing on your initiative and problem solving skills

  • Strong in project management with a focus on delivering to timings and budgets
  • Collaborative with peers across the agency to deliver award winning work.

  • Naturally, you’ll have a positive, friendly attitude, equally able to have a laugh – a large dose of banter never goes amiss at FCB
